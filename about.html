Our Company

Sun E-Ride, founded in 2008, has made significant strides in the field of electric vehicles (EVs) over the years. Initially, the company started its journey by manufacturing and launching electric three-wheelers. Since then, it has made substantial progress in the development and promotion of sustainable and eco-friendly modes of transportation.

Our Vision

Our vision at Sun-E-Ride is to become a global leader in the electric rickshaw industry, known for our innovative technology and commitment to sustainability. We strive to create a future where electric rickshaws are the preferred mode of transportation, reducing pollution and enhancing the quality of life for people in urban areas.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sun-E-Ride is to revolutionize urban transportation by manufacturing high-quality, eco-friendly electric rickshaws. We aim to provide sustainable and affordable mobility solutions that prioritize the well-being of our customers, communities, and the environment

Cruising Towards a Sustainable Future

India, with its vast and ever-changing needs, is a dynamic landscape for transportation solutions. At the heart of this dynamic environment stands a commitment to continuous innovation and the relentless pursuit of better, more sustainable mobility options.

Our journey begins with the recognition that the needs of our customers are as diverse as the nation itself. To meet these demands, we have embarked on a quest for constant improvement and evolution in our product design and technology.

One of our primary focuses has been on crafting optimized and powerful engines that cater to the need for quick and efficient commutation. We understand that time is of the essence, and our engines are meticulously designed to provide swift and reliable performance.
